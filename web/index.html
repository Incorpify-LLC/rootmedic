ENTERPRISE-GRADE TRUST

Powerful, but never reckless

RootMedic diagnoses autonomously and recommends the fix — you approve it. Every action is reviewable, reversible, and logged. No surprise commands on your servers, ever.

🙋

Human-in-the-loop

Recommend-only by default. RootMedic writes a declarative remediation.yaml — nothing executes until an operator reviews and approves.

🛡️

Secrets never leave

Every log line is scrubbed of passwords, tokens, JWTs, API keys, and PII before it reaches the LLM, an alert channel, or the archive.

🧾

Full audit trail

Each incident is archived with the diagnosis, the proposed commands, a dry-run trace, and rollback steps — with tiered retention built in.

🔌

Runs fully offline

Point it at a local Ollama model and RootMedic never phones home. Your logs stay on your own infrastructure — air-gap friendly.
